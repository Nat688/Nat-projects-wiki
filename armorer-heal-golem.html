Armorer Heal Golem

🔗 Modrinth Link

Information:

Versions: 1.21.9 - 26.1.x

Wiki

The Armorer Villager Heal Golem mod allows you to heal iron golems without having to use an ingot,
or even naturally with armorer villager within 5 blocks of the iron golem.
The iron golem will begin to heal 4 HP (2 hearts) every 60 ticks (3 seconds).



🛠️ Installation (DP):

1. Drop the .zip file into your world's datapacks folder.

2. Reload the world or use /reload.

3. Enjoy your enchanted adventure!

🛠️ Installation (MOD):

1. Drop the .jar file into the mods folder of your instance.

2. Restart your game.

3. Have fun!
