Better Crafts

Better Crafts improves Minecraft survival by adding new recipes and quality of life improvements.

Type Datapack
Version 1.21.11 - 26.1.x
Status Released

About

Better Crafts is a Minecraft datapack focused on improving the vanilla experience with additional crafting possibilities.

The goal is to make survival progression smoother while keeping the original Minecraft feeling.

Features

New Crafting Options

Adds new recipes and crafting improvements to make survival more convenient.

Vanilla Friendly

Designed to integrate naturally into Minecraft without changing the core gameplay.

Compatibility

Version Status
Minecraft 1.21.11 Supported
Minecraft 26.1.x Supported

Installation

1. Download the datapack.
2. Open your Minecraft world folder.
3. Put the datapack inside the datapacks folder.
4. Use /reload in game.

FAQ

Does it work on servers?

Yes, Better Crafts works on multiplayer servers.

Does it require a resource pack?

No, the datapack works without any resource pack.
