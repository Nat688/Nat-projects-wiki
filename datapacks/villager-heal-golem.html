Villager Heal Golem

A lightweight datapack that allows villagers to automatically heal nearby Iron Golems, making villages feel more alive while keeping the vanilla experience intact.

Type Datapack / Mod
Version 1.21.9 - 26.2
Status Released

About

Villager Heal Golem is a lightweight datapack that allows villagers to automatically repair nearby Iron Golems, helping them continue protecting their village without requiring player intervention.

Instead of manually repairing damaged golems with iron ingots, villagers will periodically restore their health, making villages feel more alive and self-sufficient while remaining faithful to vanilla Minecraft.

Features

Automatic Healing

Villagers periodically restore the health of nearby Iron Golems.

Vanilla Friendly

Fits naturally into Minecraft without changing its progression or gameplay.

Lightweight

No configuration, no commands and almost no performance impact.

Multiplayer Compatible

Works in both singleplayer and multiplayer worlds.

Commands

This datapack does not add any commands. Everything works automatically once installed.

Installation

1. Download the datapack.
2. Place it inside your world's datapacks folder.
3. Run /reload or restart the world.
4. Villagers will automatically heal nearby Iron Golems.

FAQ

Does this replace vanilla mechanics?

No. The datapack only adds automatic healing behavior while keeping vanilla gameplay unchanged.

Does it work on servers?

Yes. It works in both singleplayer and multiplayer worlds.

Does it require a resource pack?

No. The datapack works without any resource pack.
