Better Furnaces

Better Furnaces improves Minecraft survival by adding new recipes and useful quality of life improvements.

Type Datapack / Mod
Version 1.21.11 - 26.2
Status Release

About

Better Furnaces is a datapack that introduces upgradeable furnaces to Minecraft, allowing you to improve their smelting speed while preserving the vanilla gameplay experience.

Instead of replacing the existing furnace system, Better Furnaces expands it with multiple upgrade tiers, offering a natural progression as you advance through your world.

Whether you're processing large quantities of ores or simply looking for a faster and more rewarding survival experience, Better Furnaces provides a balanced solution that fits seamlessly into vanilla Minecraft.
